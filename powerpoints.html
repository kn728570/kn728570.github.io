Visually Pleasing, Thoroughly Engaging

In my opinion, being an engaging presenter is paramount to being an effective presenter. There are many facets to this, and professionally employing technology is one of the most important.

Tangible Microsoft Office and Google Classroom Training

I utilize Microsoft PowerPoint to create informative and engaging presentations. I have taken multiple post-secondary courses on making visually appealing powerpoints in an expeditious manner.

However visually striking the PowerPoint may be, it is only one aspect of an effective presentation. Using a presenter remote, I am able to remain on my feet, using my arms and body to create a stimulating lesson.
