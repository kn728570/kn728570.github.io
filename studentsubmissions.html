As an educator, I am always astounded by the work my students care capable of producing. While some of it may speak to my abilities as a teacher, I truly believe that the students are truly truly responsible for the amazing work I have received. Below are some examples of some exceptional pieces of work that I have received during my time as an educator.

An Op-Ed on the Israel/Palestine Crisis

By Uihyeok Jung

A student in my Global History 12 course at J.L. Ilsley High School in Spryfield, NS, Jung's effort and dedication was one of the highlights of my first practicum.

Using the links below, you can view Jung's submission for my formative assessment on Israel and Palestine, one of the required Case Studies in the Global History 12 curriculum.
