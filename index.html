Dependable

Punctual and organized, I can be depended on by administration to meet deadlines, handle the unexpected, and keep the classroom running smoothly. I can be depended on by the students for assistance and advice on all things inside and outside the classroom.

Knowledgeable

Fully trained to deliver curriculum based lessons in a manner consistent with modern pedagogical practices. In addition to my formal education, I strive to stay up to date on a vast number of topics in a number of different disciplines, to ensure I can always make meaningful connections when teaching.

Technologically Savvy

Self educated in all things technological. Formally educated in Microsoft Office, and 21st Century Teaching with Technology. Capable of designing visually stimulating presentations, experience working with a SmartBoard, and can act as my own tech support.

Effective Lesson Planning

Based on the Nova Scotia Curriculum, my lessons are rooted in culturally relevant pedagogy with an emphasis on objectivity. Furthermore, my lessons integrate important life skills into the classroom using the curriculum as a vessel.

I believe effective teaching is rooted in fostering community. As such, I work hard both inside and outside class time to build meaningful relationships with my students.

Professional Teaching Materials

I utilize my skills in graphic design and information technology to fabricate professional, visually striking classroom materials. I firmly believe this is necessary for engaging education.
